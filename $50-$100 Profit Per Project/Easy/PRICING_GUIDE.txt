HOW TO USE THIS GUIDE
─────────────────────
• Material costs are based on Canac / Home Depot prices in the Valleyfield, QC
  area (April 2026, CAD including Quebec taxes ~14.975%).
• Selling prices are benchmarked against active listings on:
  - Facebook Marketplace (Quebec region)
  - Kijiji Canada
  - Etsy Canada (handmade/custom category)
  - Amazon Canada (where applicable)
• Profit ranges assume you build the project yourself with basic hand & power
  tools. Labour is YOUR profit — not deducted from estimates.
• Prices are ranges. Finished quality, wood species, and custom options
  significantly affect final sale price.
• Verify lumber prices at canac.ca or your local Canac/Reno-Depot before
  purchasing — seasonal spikes (especially cedar in spring) are common.
